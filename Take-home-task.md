Take-home assignment for Entrepreneur in Residence
Your role is to act as the Product Manager for the team: analyse the market and provide
actionable recommendations on the optimal entry strategy.
The assignment is intentionally ambiguous and not clearly defined - exactly the environment in
which we expect this role to operate.
If you are lacking any information while working on the assignment, please choose the most
rational option and clearly state where and why you’ve made an assumption.
Context & Objective
We are developing a new paradigm for predictable AI-assisted development that replaces
transient chat prompts with persistent, declarative specifications. Current LLM tools struggle to
scale because chat is ephemeral and lacks architectural control; our solution treats specs as
durable artifacts that live in Git alongside the code.
By using reusable abstractions and a multi-stage translation pipeline, we "lower" high-level
concepts into implementation through editable intermediate layers. This creates a REPL-like
environment where AI-driven changes are manageable, durable, and propagate bi-directionally,
giving developers the same control over AI that they expect from a traditional compiler.
Our direct competitors include CodeSpeak, SpecKit, and Kiro. You can start your research
there.
Task Breakdown
All the questions below are meant to give you a sense of what we are looking for. Don’t think of
them as guardrails - feel free to make everything in any other way.

1. Understand the market: key concepts, saturation, key players and their positioning.
2. Create competitor overview:
a. What competitors are the most notable? Why? What can we learn/steal from
them?
b. What are existing approaches to resolving the problems? Other indirect
competitors?
3. Prepare high-level strategy:
a. What are potential niches in the market? Which are low-hanging fruits and which
are disruptive moonshots? Which one should we focus on?
b. Who will be our early-adopters? What problems / JTBDs do they have?
c. What are the best experiments to validate your strategy / hypotheses /
assumptions within the first month? Define Go/No-Go metrics if relevant.
Output
Pitch deck (pptx, Google.Slides, pdf) or one-pager adjusted for reading.
Any sources, articles, interviews, videos, etc - in any form convenient as Appendix.
